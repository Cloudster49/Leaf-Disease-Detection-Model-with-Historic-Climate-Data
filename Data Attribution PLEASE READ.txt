## Dataset Attribution

This project uses a grape leaf disease image dataset licensed under the
Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International License (CC BY-SA 4.0).

**Source:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bmshahriaalam/grape-leaf-disease
**Author(s):** B. M. Shahria Alam  
**License:** CC BY-SA 4.0  
**License URL:** https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/

The dataset was used to train and evaluate a convolutional neural network
for grape leaf disease classification. Images were resized, augmented,
and split into training and validation sets as part of the preprocessing
pipeline.
